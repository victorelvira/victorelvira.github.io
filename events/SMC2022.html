Sequential Monte Carlo Workshop (SMC 2022)

The SMC 2022 conference gathered researchers and practitioners in Sequential Monte Carlo (SMC) methods to explore the latest advancements in computational statistics and Bayesian inference. Attendees participated in lectures, discussions, and workshops covering topics such as particle filtering, importance sampling, and the diverse applications of SMC across scientific disciplines. The event promoted collaboration and knowledge sharing, focusing on how these techniques can tackle complex, high-dimensional challenges, while highlighting both practical applications and theoretical innovations in the field.

Dates: May 4–6, 2022

Location: University Carlos III of Madrid, Leganés, Spain

Organizers: Joaquín Míguez (Universidad Carlos III de Madrid) and Víctor Elvira (University of Edinburgh)

Official website: https://smc2022.webs.tsc.uc3m.es/
