Summer School on Bayesian Filtering (SSBF 2024)

The SSBF 2024 event covered essential theory and state-of-the-art methodologies in Bayesian filtering. It provided PhD students, early-career researchers, and industry professionals with comprehensive tutorials on techniques like particle filters, sigma-point methods, and Gaussian-mixture filters.

Dates: 6–10 May 2024

Location: International Centre for Mathematical Sciences (ICMS), Bayes Centre, Edinburgh, Scotland

Organizers: Víctor Elvira (University of Edinburgh), Oana Lang (Imperial College London), Joaquín Míguez (University Carlos III of Madrid), Sahani Pathiraja (UNSW Sydney)
